# LOCA Product Spec

LOCA is a place-centered social archive. Users publish photo or short-video experiences only after choosing a place, save places to `내 장소`, and turn saved places into shareable `취향 지도`.

Core loop:

1. Discover public place experiences on home or explore.
2. Open a place and read memories from different visitors.
3. Save the place with one tap.
4. Create a taste map from saved places.
5. Share a public or unlisted map with non-members.

Excluded from this MVP: likes, comments, follow graphs, DM, realtime chat, push notifications, AI recommendations, video editing, route drawing, GPX, payments, rankings, and collaborative maps.
